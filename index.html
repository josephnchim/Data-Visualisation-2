How Wasteful Are Victorians?

With fast fashion and disposable tech, the amount of waste being produced in Victoria is skyrocketing.

The Worst Contributers

This map shows which local government areas are the biggest waste producers per capita.

Bass Coast Shire is the worst culprit, with each resident producing an average of 536 kg of waste per year, followed closely by Buloke Shire producing 493 kg of waste per year. The City of Latrobe comes in 3rd at 451 kg of waste per year.

Not All Waste Is Created Equally

Garbage that is destined for landfill is more harmful to the environment than recyclables and organics. Whilst garbage is still the dominant waste type in most areas, the amount of recycling and organics in metropolitan regions is on the rise.
